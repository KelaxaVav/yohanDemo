Verification Selfie

upload a selfie of yourself while adhering to following guidelines :

  1. Clear and focused: the selfie must be sharp and not blurry.

  2. Well-Lit: use good lighting,preferably natural light,ensuring there are no shadows on your face.

  3. Face Centered: Your face should be centered in the frame.

  4. Natural Background: Use a plain, uncluttered background.

  5. No Obstructions: Ensure no hats, sunglasses, or masks are covering your face.

  6. Neutral Expression: Maintain a neutral expression avoiding extreme facial expressions.

  7. Single Person: Only your face should be visible: no other people should be in the photo.

  8. Recent Photo: The selfie should be a recent photo the accurately represents your current appearance.

  9. No Filters or Effects: Do not use any camera filters or effects.

  10. Full Face Visible: Ensure your entire face, from the top of your head to your chin, is visible in the selfie
